how to connect


- Open hexchat
- In the window it opens click on "add"
- Name the network as you prefer (ex. ft_irc) then click on "edit"
- In the new window click again on "edit" and change "newserver/6697" to "localhost/port" where port is the port you specify when launching ircserv then press enter
- Deselect the option "Use SSL for all the servers on this network"
- Click on Login method and change to "Server password"
- Inside Password put the password you specify when launching ircserv
- Click on close and in the window with all the servers choose the one you created and then click on "Connect"
